Buffalo Chicken Wings

Crispy fried chicken wings smothered in buffalo sauce

Tackle game day or any party with these amazing fried chicken wings!

Ingredients

  • Cooking oil
  • Batter
  • 3 lbs. chicken wings
  • Buffalo sauce
  • Spices

Steps:

  1. Clean wings and cover in batter and flour
  2. Fry wings for approx. 15 minutes
  3. Set to cool and cover in sauce
  4. Serve and enjoy!
