Chocolate covered Pretzels

Mini pretzels covered in elegant dark chocolate

These chocolate pretzels are a perfect light snack or delicious dessert

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces semi sweet chocolate chips
  • 12 ounces white chocolate (optional)
  • 4 ounces mini pretzel twists

Steps:

  1. Prepare baking sheet lined with parchment paper
  2. Melt the chocolate chips
  3. Dip 75% of each pretzel into melted chocolate
  4. Rest on parchment paper until cool and dried
  5. Serve and enjoy!
