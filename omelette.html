Omelette

Cheesy omelette with veggies and meats

This delicious cheesy omelette will get your day started right

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp. butter
  • 1/2 cup sharp cheddar cheese (shredded)
  • 3 slices crumbled bacon
  • 1/4 cup chopped ham

Steps:

  1. Combine eggs in a bowl, whisk until beaten well
  2. Melt butter in nonstick skillet over medium heat
  3. Add egg mixture
  4. Cook for 3-4 minutes until set
  5. Sprinkle cheese,bacon and ham onto half the omelette
  6. Fold omelette in half, cook 1 more minute
  7. Serve and enjoy!
